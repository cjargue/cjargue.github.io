Western PA ARML

Since 2016 I have been privileged to coach the Western Pennsylvania ARML Team, a Pittsburgh-area math team for high school and middle school students. The team practices each year for the American Regions Math League (ARML), and competes in the annual competition at Penn State University the first weekend of June. Members of the team compete in many other math competitions as well--local and national, individual and team. Practices are held weekly on CMU's campus.
